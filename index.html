Western Optical Transient Survey

WOTS

Western Optical Transient Survey consists of a 0.33-m telescope + CCD which robotically scans the Northern Hemisphere every clear night as a part of an international collaboration project called Global SuperNovae Search Team.

Global SuperNovae Search Team

GSNST

Global SuperNovae Search Team is an International collaboration between Astronomers and Amateur Astronomers to search and study optical transients using several robotic observatories, including the 1.83-m Plaskett Telescope in Victoria, BC, Canada.
